How it's calculated
NOI Y1 = (Lease Rate − Opex) × Area × 12  ·  Purchase Price = NOI Y1 ÷ Entry Cap  ·  Exit Value = Escalated NOI ÷ Exit Cap
Loan = Purchase Price × LTV  ·  Annual Principal = Loan ÷ Repayment Period  ·  Interest = Opening Balance × Interest Rate
Levered CF = NOI − Principal − Interest  ·  IRR = rate where NPV of all cashflows = 0 (XIRR)
