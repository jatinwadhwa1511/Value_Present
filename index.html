How it's calculated
Hard Cost = Σ(per sqm items) × Area  ·  Soft Cost = Hard Cost × Σ(soft % items)  ·  Annual Cost = (Hard + Soft) × Phase% each year
Annual Drawdown = Annual Cost × LTC  ·  Construction Interest = rolled into loan balance (PIK)  ·  Peak Debt = cumulative drawn + accrued interest
Operation Start = last construction year + 1  ·  NOI = (Rent − Opex) × Area × 12, escalating each year  ·  Exit Value = final NOI (escalated) ÷ Exit Cap
Unlevered IRR = XIRR(−Land Y0, −construction costs each yr, NOI + Exit)  ·  Levered IRR = XIRR(−equity each yr, levered CF, Exit)

How it's calculated
Entry EBITDA = Revenue × EBITDA Margin  ·  Entry EV = Entry EBITDA × Entry Multiple  ·  Year N EBITDA = Revenue × (1+g)^N − COGS − SG&A + Synergies
Debt at Year N = Initial Debt − (Initial Debt × Amort × N)  ·  Interest = Opening Debt × Rate  ·  Exit Equity = Exit EV − Debt at Exit
PE/Seller Split = PE Equity ÷ (PE Equity + Rollover)  ·  Fund Proceeds = Exit Equity × PE Split
Hurdle = Fund Equity × ((1+pref)^Hold − 1)  ·  Catch-up = 100% to GP until GP/LP ratio above pref = carry split  ·  Above Catch-up = LP/GP carry split
